Equipment




A Table Tennis racket has 2 main parts that you should know about, the blade and the rubber. What's so fun about this sport is that you can easily change your equipment to fit your playing syle. If you prefer to play more controlled, you can get a Stiga All-Around Classic for the blade and a Yasaka Mark V for the rubber. Or, if you want to deal more powerful shots, you can get a Butterfly Viscaria for the blade and a Butterfly Dignics 09c for the rubber. The world of equipment is vast, so make sure to find the ones that fit you most.
