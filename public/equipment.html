Equipment




A Table Tennis racket has 2 main parts that you should know about, the blade and the rubber. What's so fun about this sport is that you can easily change your equipment to fit your playing syle. There are rackets, blades and rubbers for everyone, whether offensive or defensive. The world of equipment is vast, so make sure to find the ones that fit you most.
